Happy New Year, and welcome to 2026 🎉

After a short break, we’re officially back in **active prototyping** and moving forward with the next iteration of the Notificator Project device.

We’d like to share a quick update regarding the **closed beta** timeline. Closed beta devices are now planned to ship in **late February**.

There’s a small delay compared to our original estimate, caused by an improvement we decided to make to the prototype. This update affects **both hardware and software**, and while it added a bit of extra time, it results in a more robust and reliable device overall. We believe this is the right call.

Thank you for the continued interest and support. More updates coming very soon 🚀
